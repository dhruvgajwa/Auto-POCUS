POCUS
A SOLUTION TO ENABLE UNIVERSAL COVERAGE OF ‘POINT-OF-CARE’ ULTRASOUND DIAGNOSIS, EVEN IN THE REMOTEST PARTS OF THE WORLD
POCUS, or Point-of-Care Ultrasonography, refers to a form of diagnostic procedure that can be brought to the patient’s side – whether in emergency, outpatient, inpatient, intensive care settings, even in the back of an ambulance – and be used in real time to gather data and make meaningful clinical decisions.
